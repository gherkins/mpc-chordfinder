This is a tool to help you locate chords on your AKAI MPC or any other 12/16-pads

• Select a single note sample (poly mode)
• Tune it so that PAD1 is C
• Hit 16/12-LEVELS (tuning)
• Grab your real book and get ready
• Play some chords on your pads
